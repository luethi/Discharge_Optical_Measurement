#Welcome to the Discharge technology! measure Discharge with Webcam or Smartphone

On these pages we explain to you how you can measure the discharge of a river, stream, furrow or channel with our image based ‘Discharge’ technology. The technology is based on imaging your river with either of:

  • Fixed IP cameras and our system ‘DischargeKeeper’, available through photrack, send us an email for a quote.
  • Smartphone and our ‘Android App’ ‘Discharge’, available on google play.
    Both tools are integrated with our web-backend site discharge.ch.
