Welcome to the Discharge technology!

Discharge with Webcam or Smartphone - accurate optical measurements

These pages explain how you can measure the discharge of a river, stream, furrow or channel with our image based Discharge technology. The technology is based on imaging your river with either of:

  • DischargeKeeper, for fixed IP cameras, available through photrack, ask for a quote.
  • Discharge app for Android, available on google play.

Both tools are integrated with our web-backend site discharge.ch.
